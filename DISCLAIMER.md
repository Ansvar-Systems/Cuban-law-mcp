## Data Authority and Limitations

### Source Authority

This Tool aggregates data from official Cuban legal databases. However:

| Source Type | Authority Level | Use Case |
|------------|----------------|----------|
| **Gaceta Oficial de Cuba (gacetaoficial.cu)** | Authoritative | Statute text, decrees, regulations (primary reference) |
| **Tribunal Supremo Popular** | Authoritative | Supreme court jurisprudence |
| **Community/open-data sources** | Supplementary | Cross-references, metadata (verify independently) |

**CRITICAL**: Community-maintained resources are **NOT** official government publications. While generally reliable, they may contain transcription errors, outdated information, or incomplete coverage.

**ADDITIONAL CAUTION**: Cuban law has undergone significant structural reform. The 2019 Constitution (Constitución de la República de Cuba) and subsequent legislation have substantially changed the legal framework. Verify that all provisions you consult reflect post-2019 constitutional reforms.

### Commercial Legal Databases

For professional legal work, consult institutional repositories maintained by Cuban universities (Universidad de La Habana) and official ONBC legal resources, which provide editorial oversight that this Tool **cannot** provide.

## Currency and Staleness

### No Real-Time Updates

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by **weeks or months**
- Cuban law changes through new statutes (leyes), decree-laws (decretos-leyes), council of ministers decrees (decretos del Consejo de Ministros), and resolutions (resoluciones)
- **Last-updated timestamps** in tool responses indicate data age, but should be treated as unreliable

### Staleness Warnings

The Tool includes staleness warnings when data is >30 days old. **These warnings are not exhaustive**:

- Even "fresh" data may be outdated if recent amendments were published in the Gaceta Oficial
- Staleness warnings do not guarantee completeness or accuracy
- Always check official sources for the current legal position (texto legal vigente)

### Amendments and Repeals

- A provision marked "in force" may have been:
  - Amended (modificado) after the last database update
  - Repealed (derogado) but not yet removed from the database
  - Subject to transitional rules (disposiciones transitorias) affecting its applicability
- The Tool does **NOT** automatically track amendments in real-time

## Coverage Gaps

### What's Missing

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Complete case law** -- Coverage of Tribunal Supremo Popular and lower court decisions is limited; do not rely solely on this for jurisprudence research
2. **Historical statute versions** -- Limited availability of historical provision wording before and after the 2019 constitutional reform
3. **Legal commentary** -- No editorial annotations, academic commentary (doctrina), or practice guides
4. **Unpublished decisions** -- Lower court decisions and administrative rulings largely absent
5. **Full legislative history** -- Limited coverage of Asamblea Nacional materials
6. **Regulatory instruments** -- Ministerial resolutions and sector-specific regulatory instruments may be missing or incomplete

### Impact on Professional Use

These gaps mean:

- **Incomplete Legal Research**: Tool results are inherently incomplete and may miss critical authorities
- **Context Missing**: Without commentary and legislative materials, interpretation may be incorrect
- **Reform Complexity**: The interaction between pre-2019 and post-2019 legal instruments requires expert judgment that this Tool cannot supply
